Frequently Asked Questions (or FAQs)

Q#1 Is it safe to swim in irradiated water?

Some of our top scientists have discovered through trial and error, (our lawyers have advised us to not delve further) that coming into contact with irradiated water tests the body's natural ability to absorb and transform radiation into usable energy.

Irradiated water also holds the potential to boost cellular regeneration, increase resistance to other environmental toxins, and enhance physical stamina. Additionally, it can be seen as a means to strengthen mutant community bonds, with those who share these extraordinary adaptations, fostering a sense of unity and resilience. No relation to the Master's Unity.

Q#2 Are there any group discounts?

For starters, we accept virtually any type of currency and/or payment. Due to fluctuating currencies and the Brotherhood-Institute skirmishes, your currency value is pro-rated by our expert staff. We take Pre-War money (cash, in bulk), Gold Bullion, Gold Bars, bulk scrap or miner scrip for our Appalachian friends.

Whether you're a band of scavengers looking to bond over a shared thrill or a family unit seeking a memorable escape from the harsh realities of the wasteland, our group packages cater to parties of all sizes. For groups of five or more, we offer a 20% discount on our standard rates, ensuring everyone can experience the excitement without breaking the bank. Additionally, larger groups will have the added advantage of a discounted (20%) guide and exclusive access to our most secure rafts, providing an extra layer of safety and camaraderie.

Q#3 What safety measures are in place?

Our rafts are constructed from the finest scraps scavenged and expertly patched together with duct tape and determination. We pride ourselves on our innovative approach to safety; our life jackets, stitched from tire rubber and stuffed with dried mut-fruit, offer buoyancy and a bit of protection against the elements. Each raft is equipped with a mounted .50 cal, perfect for fending off any unexpected super mutants that may come your way.

Our guides are seasoned survivors, having navigated these treacherous waters countless times with only a few incidents. Each carries signal flares, first-aid supplies, freshly salvaged from Boston Medical, and a hardly Plasma Caster. We believe that part of the adventure is embracing the unknown, so while our safety measures may seem unconventional, they are tailored to the unique challenges of our Post-War world. At White Rad Rafting, we guarantee that your rafting experience will be as unforgettable as the apocalypse itself.
